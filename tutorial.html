Get Started

How to use?

Primary Step: Start Small | Understand your present financial state

  • Open Calculator

  • Provide a starting balance. This is the amount of money you will start with from the first month to initiate the starting of your savings or investment account

  • Provide the maximum and minimum saving top-up, interest, and profit percentages. This is the amount of profit you wish to put in each month. For example, Wealthsimple is currently providing 4-5% interest on your regular banking account. You can start with 4-5% as an example that is already available at your disposal.

  • See the result with your ending balance in a 1-year, 2-year, and 3-year terms

Use Calculator >>

Final Step: Be Consistent | Reach Million

  • At the very bottom of the results, a form is asking for a booster amount. Without a booster amount, the result may not be very realistic with the current economic infrastructure. This is the amount of money you will put as an additional amount each month in your savings or investment account

  • Provide a starting balance. This is the amount of money you will start with your first month to put in your savings or investment account

  • Provide the maximum and minimum saving top-up, interest, and profit percentages. This is the amount of profit you wish to put in each month. For example, Wealthsimple is currently providing 4-5% interest on your regular banking account. You can start with 4-5% as an example that is already available at your disposal.

  • See the result with your ending balance in 1-year, 2-year, and 3-year terms

  • Press the "LMillion" button to reveal the result of the millionaire trajectory
